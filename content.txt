This solution contains solutions to problems presented in the competitive programming course, given at the Interdiscplinary Center (IDC), Herzliya.
Solutions are pulled from the course moodle page.

Motivation:
Providing a concentrated view of solutions to presented problems. This is helpful in finding comonly used techniques.

Structure:
Problems are divided by two levels:
1. Topic: Each topic, we learn a new subject.
2. Problem location: Problems are presented during a lesson, or during a tournament. A 3rd option was added: Practice. Here you can put your own files.
